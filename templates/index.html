Two-Phase Analysis Process

Phase 1: H&E to IHC Conversion

Using advanced Pix2Pix GAN models, we convert standard H&E (Hematoxylin and Eosin) stained tissue slides into virtual IHC (Immunohistochemical) images.

  • Non-destructive tissue analysis
  • Cost-effective alternative to traditional IHC
  • Preserves tissue integrity

Phase 2: Cancer Severity Prediction

Advanced CNN classifiers analyze the generated virtual IHC images to predict cancer severity, HER2 expression levels, and generate comprehensive diagnostic reports.

  • HER2 expression analysis
  • Biomarker quantification
  • Automated report generation
